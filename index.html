About CCL CSR

Coal for the Nation.
Care for the Community.

"CCL's CSR is not philanthropy — it is a commitment to shared prosperity with the communities that have given us coal."

Central Coalfields Limited operates 17 mines across Jharkhand, extracting coal that powers the nation. Our CSR programme is our covenant with the communities that host these operations — investing their land's wealth back into their futures.

Governed by the Companies Act 2013 and the CSR Policy approved by our Board, we direct 2% of average net profit toward programmes that strengthen education, healthcare, livelihoods and infrastructure in CCL's command area.

⛏️
17 operational mines across Jharkhand's coalfields form our primary area of CSR responsibility
🏥
14 mobile health units reach remote villages with free primary healthcare and maternal services
🏛️
Board-level CSR Committee chaired by Independent Director with quarterly review and public disclosure
🎯
2 aspirational districts — Latehar and Palamu — receive priority allocation under Government of India's framework
