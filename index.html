About CCL

A Mini-Ratna Powering India's Growth

Central Coalfields Limited, a Mini-Ratna subsidiary of Coal India Limited, extracted 87.50 Million Tonnes in FY 2024–25 and contributed ₹4,300 crore to Jharkhand through royalties, DMF, and taxes. Our CSR programme channels this strength into the communities we operate within.

⛏️

38 Active Mines across 6 Coalfields & 14 Areas

3 Underground & 35 Opencast mines, 5 Washeries spanning the length of Jharkhand.

🏥

Comprehensive In-house Healthcare

4 Central Hospitals, 8 Area Hospitals, 26 Dispensaries, 694 beds and 73 ambulances.

📋

Board-driven, Rule-compliant CSR Governance

35 CD Cadre officers, independent impact assessments by TERI & IICA, CAG audit with zero paras.

🗺️

All 8 Districts are NITI Aayog Aspirational Districts

Chatra, Latehar, Palamu, Giridih, Hazaribagh, Bokaro, Ranchi and Ramgarh — among India's 112 Aspirational Districts.

Voices from the Field

The People Behind the Numbers

Every statistic represents a real person whose life has changed. These are the communities at the heart of CCL's ₹81.11 crore annual investment.

🎓

From a Tailor's Son to Amazon Engineer

Wards of farmers, labourers, tailors and mechanics — coached by IITian CCL officers — now work at Amazon, Microsoft, SAP Labs and L&T, inspiring the next generation from villages and small towns across Jharkhand.

📍 CCL Ke Lal & Laadli · DAV Gandhinagar, Ranchi

❤️

82 Children with Second Chances at Life

Under Nanha Sa Dil, 30,768 children have been screened for Congenital Heart Disease across 5 districts. 82 have received free cardiac surgery — hospitalization, travel and post-operative care fully covered by CCL.

📍 Latehar, Hazaribagh, Ramgarh, Giridih, Palamu

⚡

Ten Women Who Became Breadwinners

Ten underprivileged tribal women in Ranchi received electric auto-rickshaws, driving licences and technical training. Each now earns ₹15,000 per month — serving patients at RK Mission TB Sanatorium.

📍 RK Mission, Ranchi · E-Rickshaw Initiative
