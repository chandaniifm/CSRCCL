CCL Ke Lal & CCL Ki Laadli

Free JEE Advanced-level engineering entrance coaching — with schooling, hostel, meals, sports, and medical care — for underprivileged children from within 25 km of CCL establishments. Taught by IITian CCL officers. Running since 2012.

"Wards of farmers, labourers, tailors, mechanics, and rural shop owners — working today at Amazon, Microsoft, SAP Labs, and L&T. Inspiring every aspirant from villages and small towns of Jharkhand."

— CCL Annual Report 2024–25 · CCL Ke Lal & CCL Ki Laadli

What Every Student Receives — Free of Charge

1

JEE Advanced-Level Coaching by IITian Officers

CCL's own IITian employees serve as faculty — delivering elite coaching at no cost to students or the company beyond salaries already on payroll.

2

+2 Schooling at DAV Gandhinagar, Ranchi

Students are enrolled in one of Ranchi's premier CBSE schools, ensuring their board results match their entrance coaching quality.

3

Hostel with 24×7 Security

Secure, supervised residential accommodation on campus — giving students from distant villages a safe and stable base.

4

Nutritious & Wholesome Meals

Three meals a day — the only outsourced component of the entire programme — ensuring no student studies on an empty stomach.

5

Sports Facilities & Medical Care

Access to CCL's sports infrastructure and healthcare — managing the physical and mental wellbeing of students through a demanding 2-year programme.

6

Motivation & Mentoring

CCL employees serve as wardens, supervisors, and bus drivers — creating a tight-knit community around each cohort of students.

Selection Process

Open to 10th pass students, family income <₹8 lakh/year, residing within 25 km of CCL establishments.

Project Digital Vidya

Smart Classrooms and ICT Labs installed in 193 government schools across all 8 CCL operational districts of Jharkhand — implemented through EdCIL, a Government of India enterprise under the Ministry of Education.

Installation Complete · 3-Year AMC Active

193 Schools. 8 Districts.
One Digital Revolution.

Every school was individually vetted by EdCIL for feasibility — +2 schools with more than 100 students and confirmed internet connectivity. The final school list was approved by District Collectors of all 8 districts. Installation and teacher training was completed and inaugurated on 24 January 2025.

₹26.13 Cr

State Library, Ranchi

A landmark public institution for Jharkhand — a GRIHA 4-star rated, six-floor library on 2 acres of land in Ranchi. Built under MoU with the Department of School Education, Government of Jharkhand. Open to all citizens of the state.

90% Structure Complete · Finishing in Progress

CCL's single largest educational infrastructure investment — ₹65.25 crore (₹30 crore by CIL), with a contract awarded at ₹55 crore (excluding books). Construction was delayed due to forest permission requirements for tree removal and dismantling of the old building, with ground-breaking in March 2024. The Government of Jharkhand's Secretary of Education has requested extension of the MoU to 31 March 2026.

🏛️

Six Floors, 11,753 Sq. Metres

Spread across 2 acres of land in Ranchi — a landmark public building for the state capital.

📚

Reading Halls & E-Resource Section

Separate reading halls, a dedicated e-resources and journals section, and a digital library zone.

🎓

Group Study & Cubicles

Cubicles for individual focus, group study rooms, conference rooms, and meeting rooms throughout.

☕

Cafeteria & Meditation Centre

A cafeteria and meditation centre — making the library a full-day destination, not just a reference stop.

♻️

GRIHA 4-Star Green Rating

Solar rooftop power, sewage treatment plant, and sustainable design achieving GRIHA 4-star certification.

🔒

Full Smart Infrastructure

CCTV, data networking, access control, fire alarm systems, and modular furniture throughout all six floors.

More Education Initiatives

Also Funded by CCL CSR

Beyond the three flagship programmes, CCL supports a range of education-adjacent initiatives that extend access, improve quality, and build lasting infrastructure.

Centralised Kitchen, Ramgarh

A ₹22.09 crore kitchen to deliver mid-day meals to 50,000 students across 538 government schools in Ramgarh district — built in partnership with Akshaya Patra Foundation. Structure 80% complete.

₹22.09 Cr · 50,000 Students
💻

Business Computer Applications — CIA Express

Implemented by ICA Foundation, this programme trained 100 SC/ST underprivileged students from Ranchi college campuses in Business Computer Applications, Accounting, ERP and GST — with 100% placement guarantee and all-time job cards.

100% Placed · SC/ST Beneficiaries
🏫

School Health Camps

CCL doctors conduct dedicated health camps in government schools within command areas — screening for anaemia, vision, dental and general health issues, with referral services provided.
